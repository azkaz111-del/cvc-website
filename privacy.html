Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Cardiovascular Consultants P.C. ("CVC," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of our patients and website visitors. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you visit this website. It is provided for general informational purposes and is separate from our Notice of Privacy Practices (NPP), which governs your Protected Health Information under HIPAA.

Notice of Privacy Practices (HIPAA)

The handling of your Protected Health Information (PHI) as a patient of our practice is governed by our formal Notice of Privacy Practices, in accordance with the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA). A copy is available at each of our office locations and can be downloaded from our New Patient Forms page. If you have questions about the NPP, please contact our office.

Information Collected on This Website

This website is primarily informational. We do not collect Protected Health Information through this website, and this website does not include a form for submitting personal health details. We may collect limited, non-identifying information automatically, such as:

  • Standard technical data (browser type, device type, general location) collected by our hosting provider for security and performance
  • Aggregate, anonymous usage statistics

We do not sell, rent, or trade any information collected through this website.

Contacting Us

If you contact us by phone or email using the information on our Contact page, we use that information solely to respond to your inquiry. Please do not include sensitive personal health information in email, as standard email is not a secure method of communication. To discuss your health information securely, please call our office or use the patient portal.

Patient Portal & Third-Party Services

Our patient portal and online bill pay are provided by third-party services (FollowMyHealth and FMH Notify). Your use of those services is governed by their respective privacy policies and security practices. Links to external websites are provided for convenience; we are not responsible for the privacy practices of other sites.

Cookies

This website uses minimal cookies necessary for basic functionality and security. We do not use advertising cookies.

Children's Privacy

This website is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children through the website.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at azito@cvcpc.com or (586) 698-1200.

This Privacy Policy is provided as a general template and should be reviewed by the practice's legal counsel or compliance officer to ensure it accurately reflects the practice's actual data practices and complies with all applicable federal and Michigan state law.
