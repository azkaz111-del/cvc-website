Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Cardiovascular Consultants P.C. ("CVC," "we," "us," or "our") respects the privacy of our patients and website visitors. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected through this website and how it is used. It is separate from our HIPAA Notice of Privacy Practices, which governs your Protected Health Information as a patient of the practice.

Who We Are

Our website address is https://cvc-website-smoky.vercel.app. For questions about this policy, contact us at azito@cvcpc.com or (586) 698-1200.

Notice of Privacy Practices (HIPAA)

The handling of your Protected Health Information (PHI) as a patient is governed by our formal Notice of Privacy Practices under the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA). A copy is available at each of our office locations. Please do not send Protected Health Information through this website or by standard email, as email is not a secure method of communication.

What Personal Data We Collect and Why

This website is primarily informational. We do not sell, rent, or trade your information.

Contact by phone or email

If you contact us using the phone numbers or email addresses listed on the site, we use that information solely to respond to your inquiry.

Media

If images are uploaded to the website, visitors can download and extract any location data (EXIF GPS) included in those images. We avoid publishing images with embedded location data.

Embedded content from other websites

Some pages (such as physician profiles or news articles) may include embedded content — for example, Instagram posts, videos, or maps. Embedded content from other websites behaves exactly as if you had visited the other website. These websites may collect data about you, use cookies, embed additional third-party tracking, and monitor your interaction with that embedded content, including tracking your interaction if you have an account and are logged in to that website.

Cookies

This website uses only minimal cookies necessary for basic functionality and security. We do not use advertising cookies.

Analytics

We may collect aggregate, anonymized statistics (such as page views) to understand how the site is used and to improve it. This data does not identify individual visitors.

How Long We Retain Your Data

Inquiries you send us are retained only as long as needed to respond and for our ordinary business records. We do not maintain public user accounts or a comment system on this website.

What Rights You Have Over Your Data

You may request to receive a copy of the personal data we hold about you that you have provided to us, and you may request that we erase it. This does not include data we are obliged to keep for administrative, legal, or security purposes, including medical records governed by HIPAA and applicable state law.

Where We Send Your Data

Our patient portal and online bill pay are provided by third-party services (FollowMyHealth and FMH Notify). Your use of those services is governed by their own privacy policies and security practices.

Connect With Us

Follow the practice on social media: Facebook and Instagram.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

This Privacy Policy is provided as a general template and should be reviewed by the practice's legal counsel or compliance officer to ensure it accurately reflects the practice's actual data practices and complies with all applicable federal and Michigan state law.
