About Dr. Ali Novitsky

Why we brought her program in-house

Dr. Ali Novitsky is a triple board-certified physician (Obesity Medicine, Pediatrics, and Neonatology) and a Master Certified Coach, known for her work at the intersection of muscle preservation, metabolic health, and sustainable behavior change.

Her approach is grounded in clinical physiology, psychological safety, and real-world adherence — not fitness trends or extremes. She created these programs to solve a problem clinicians see every day: patients are told to "strength train," but rarely given a system that works with their physiology, schedule, and emotional capacity.

About the Role of These Programs in Your Care

A path to strength alongside your medical care

Beginner Strength & Mindset and Total Strength & Mindset are offered as optional wellness resources. They exist to provide a clear, physician-designed path to strength alongside ongoing medical care.

They are not medical treatment. They are not insurance-reimbursable. They do not replace physician care.
