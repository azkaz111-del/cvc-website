Who We Are

About Cardiovascular Consultants

An advanced approach to heart and vascular healthcare that's comprehensive, compassionate and convenient — for more than 30 years.

Our Mission

"To provide knowledge, encouragement, and the finest individual therapy — so you become an active participant in your own care."

Cardiovascular Consultants is a dedicated group of physicians who have devised a multi-disciplinary approach for prevention and treatment of cardiac and vascular disease.

CVC has been providing patients with the most advanced treatment and therapy for more than 30 years, with emphasis on:

  • Primary Prevention — Reduce to a minimum the likelihood of threatening cardiovascular adverse events.
  • Secondary Prevention — Minimize the potential of recurring cardiovascular complications.

In addition to distinguished academic and clinical achievements, our physicians are actively involved in clinical research — spearheading the use of advanced therapies in nationally recognized institutions.

Comprehensive Care

A Unique Entity for Your
Cardiac & Vascular Care

It is the comprehensive approach that makes Cardiovascular Consultants a unique entity for your cardiac and vascular care. Should your condition require more immediate attention, our clinics provide:

  • State-of-the-Art Diagnostic Testing
  • Advanced, evidence-based medical management
  • The finest and latest cardiac and vascular interventional procedures

We have combined the latest technologies and expertise to offer expert cardiac and vascular care by board-certified cardiologists, exercise physiologists, and cardiac and vascular technologists.

Our physicians work hand-in-hand with your primary care physician and your preferred hospital. Our role in advancing care is twofold:

  • First — enhance our own knowledge and our medical community's awareness of new medical advances.
  • Second — introduce these advances into our patients' therapy.

Your cardiac and vascular health is a mutual responsibility — yours and ours. Our programs are tailored to address and modify your specific risk factors, enabling you to enjoy the highest quality of life.
