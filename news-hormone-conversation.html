The Hormone Conversation
We Never Had

November 23, 2025 · Cardiovascular Consultants

Join us for "The Hormone Conversation We Never Had," a powerful and informative evening dedicated to women's health and the role hormones play in overall well-being. This event brings together leading medical experts to address the questions, challenges, and misunderstandings many women face throughout their health journey.

Event Details

Date & Time: Thursday, December 11 · 7:00–8:30 PM
Location: Danialle's Clubhouse, Birmingham, MI
Tickets: Available starting November 25

Featured Speakers

  • Dr. LeAnne Roberts, MD, FACOG, NCMP
  • Dr. Sindhu Koshy, MD, FACC, NCMP — Cardiovascular Consultants
  • Dr. Carrie Leff, DO, NCMP
  • Danialle Karmanos, Clubhouse Founder

Presented by MadDog Technology and Henry Ford Health.

This essential conversation aims to empower women with knowledge, confidence, and the ability to advocate for their own health.
